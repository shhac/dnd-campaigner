## Using This File

### For the GM
- Reference when NPCs react to the party
- Set social check DCs based on disposition
- Create hooks based on relationships
- Remember past interactions for consistency

### For AI Players
- May receive relevant excerpts about their character's known relationships
- Use to inform reactions to NPCs and party members

### Update Triggers
- After significant social encounters
- After betrayals, rescues, or sacrifices
- After gifts, favors, or insults
- When trust is gained or lost
- At session end if anything changed

### Disposition Effects on Play

| Disposition | Social DC Modifier | NPC Behavior |
|-------------|-------------------|--------------|
| Hostile | +10 | Active opposition, may attack |
| Unfriendly | +5 | Uncooperative, minimum service |
| Neutral | +0 | Transactional, no extra effort |
| Friendly | -2 | Helpful, shares information |
| Allied | -5 | Goes out of their way, significant favors |
